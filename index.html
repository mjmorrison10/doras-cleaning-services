Our Services

Affordable Cleaning Services
For Every Home

Whether you need a one-time deep clean or regular weekly service, Dora's Cleaning has you covered. Every service is thorough, every price is fair, and every customer matters.

Residential Cleaning

Complete home cleaning tailored to your needs. Kitchens, bathrooms, bedrooms, living areas. We follow a detailed checklist so nothing gets missed. Your home, only cleaner.

Deep Cleaning

When regular cleaning is not enough. We tackle built-up grime, neglected areas, and hard-to-reach spots. Baseboards, behind appliances, inside cabinets. A true deep clean.

Move-In/Move-Out

Moving? Let us handle the cleaning so you can focus on the move. We make sure every room is spotless for the next occupant or ready for you to move in fresh.

Recurring Service Plans

Weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly cleaning at discounted rates. Consistent, reliable service from the same team every visit. Your home stays clean without you lifting a finger.

Kitchen & Bathroom Sanitizing

The two rooms that need the most attention. We sanitize countertops, sinks, tubs, showers, and toilets. Remove soap scum, hard water stains, and bacteria. Fresh and clean.

Dusting & Vacuuming

Comprehensive dusting of all surfaces, furniture, and fixtures. Thorough vacuuming of carpets, rugs, and hard floors. We even dust the places you forget about.

Laundry Service

Washing, drying, and folding laundry so you do not have to. Bed linens, towels, and everyday clothing. Spend your weekends doing things you enjoy instead of laundry.

Organization Help

Need help getting your home in order? We assist with organizing closets, pantries, garages, and living spaces. A tidy home is a peaceful home. Let us help you get there.
